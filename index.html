Average City MPG VS Average HighWay MPG

Different Graphs

This graph is taken and modified from the activity. In order to showcase our data from my csv file, "https://flunky.github.io/cars2017.csv", I have created this graph which shows each data value's Average Highway MPG and Average City MPG, with the City being on the X axis and the Highway being on the Y axis. As you can see, there seems to be a direct correlation between both MPGs, with each of them increasing as the other value also increases. There also seems to be a trend in which the less Engine Cylinders a vehicle has, the higher MPG they get as well. Furthermore there seems to have a strong correlation between the gas a vehicle uses with its MPG.
