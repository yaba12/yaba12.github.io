Fuel Type Vs Average Highway MPG/h1>

Here we display a bar graph that shows the Average High MPG each fuel type of car is able to obtain. On the x Axis, we group all the data based on its fuel type, and Average all the AverageHighwayMPG in our csv and display that into our y axis. As you can see, there is a strong indication that electric cars have the best MPG while driving on the highway. On the other hand, Disel and Gasoline based cars do not get a good ratio.
